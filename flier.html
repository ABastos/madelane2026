About
Big Data in Neuroscience.
Methods to Match.

Recent advances in recording now permit researchers to measure from hundreds to thousands of sites across cortical and sub-cortical areas in animal models and human patients. MaDeLaNe presents the latest state-of-the-art answers to analyzing these datasets — from spike sorting and laminar analysis to population coding and human neurophysiology. Participants learn through hands-on, data-driven tutorials led by leading experts.
