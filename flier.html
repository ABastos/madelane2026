About
Big Data in Neuroscience.
Methods to Match.

MaDeLaNe — Multi-Area, high-Density, Laminar Neurophysiology — is a hands-on workshop presenting state-of-the-art methods for analyzing large-scale neural data. Advances in recording now permit measurements from hundreds to thousands of sites across cortical and sub-cortical areas in animal models and human patients. Participants learn through data-driven tutorials covering spike sorting, laminar analysis, population coding, and human neurophysiology.
